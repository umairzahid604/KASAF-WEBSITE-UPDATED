What We Have Done

KASAF-LAGOS OUTREACH, NOVEMBER 9TH, 2020:

KASAF-LAGOS Outreach on Sunday November 8th, 2020 at Ipaja, Lagos. It was an amazing time of blessing for 64 families who each received a blessing pack containing rice, beans, Indomie, garri and tomatoes. A total of 200 people benefited from the generosity of our partners and sponsors. It was indeed an avalanche of blessing. Thank you our dear partners and sponsors for your immense support, prayers, encouragement and funding. We greatly appreciate you . God bless you.

KASAF-KENYA Outreach 28th of October 2020 at Mai Mahiu (hot waters), Naivasha County, Kenya.
