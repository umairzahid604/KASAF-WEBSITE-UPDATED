WHAT WE HAVE DONE

KASAF WARU-ABUJA, NIGERIA JULY 2021 BIRTHDAY CELEBRATION

On Saturday June 26th, 2021, KASAF VOLUNTEERS set out to celebrate June Birthday celebrants at Charity Orphanage Home, Apo with Beji Gofwen Peter (KASAF-WARU Volunteer June Birthday celebrant). It was indeed a great day of celebration as 6 KASAF volunteers arrived at the Orphanage Home to celebrate with 18 June celebrants at the 100 Kid's Orphanage Home. Items brought included Indomie, rolls of bar soap, spaghetti, milk juice and detergents. Fun activities such as dancing, prayers and interactive adventures were done with the celebrants. It was indeed an amazing time of celebrations and fun. Thank you dear Volunteers and sponsors for celebrating with the children. God bless you.


KASAF FOOD OUTREACH, DNAKO VILLAGE, BWARI-ABUJA, NIGERIA on 13th July 2021.

KASAF FOOD OUTREACH at Dnako Village, Bwari-Abuja was a great time of blessing for the community. Food items were brought and distributed to the community and these included Rice, Garri and Indomie noodles. We reached out to 108 families, and 29 children. With a total of 138 food packs distributed. A gift of 1 carton of indomie, 3modules of gari, and 3modules of rice was also given to the Traditonal Ruler (the chief) in appreciation of his efforts to ensure his community receives the kind gesture form KASAF. Thank you dear Volunteers and sponsors for always blessing our communities. God bless you


KASAF WARU-ABUJA INTERNATIONAL YOUTH DAY CELEBRATIONS 2021

On Thursday 12th of August 2021, KASAF volunteers organized a football tournament for youths of the Waru community, in commemoration of the International Youth Day Celebrations 2021. The event was held at Waru Primary School Field, Waru, Abuja.  It was indeed a great time of celebration for the youths as cash prizes were given to the teams and their members present, food, drinks and fun activities were done to mark the celebration.   Thank you dear volunteers and partners.   God bless you


KASAF BARANGONI- ABUJA INTERNATIONAL YOUTH DAY CELEBRATIONS 2021

On Friday 13th of August 2021, KASAF volunteers organized a celebration for the youths of Barangoni community. Diverse lectures were given to educate the youths on Nation Building and Success as a Youth. Topics discussed include Health Awareness, Social Media Engagement and monetization as a youth in the 21st Century.   It was a great time of enlightenment for the youths. Food items, snacks and drinks were also distributed to all the youths present to commemorate the day. It was a resounding success.   Thank you dear volunteers and partners for your relentless efforts in creating sustainable development in our communities.


KASAF KENYA INTERNATIONAL YOUTH DAY CELEBRATIONS 2021

On Saturday August 14th 2021, the Volunteers of KASAF Kenya set out to commemorate the International Youth Day 2021 with the youths of Mai Mahiu, Naivasha County, Kenya.   In collaboration with Christian Outreach Mai Mahiu, the youths in the community came out to celebrate the day. Diverse activities were performed such as Talent Shows, Team Building workshops, Empowerment & Motivational Sessions, praise and worship and of course, Lunch and refreshments were provided to all youths present.   It was a great time of celebrations for the youths.   Thank you dear volunteers and partners for your continuous and un-relented efforts in impacting our communities. God bless you.


KASAF in collaboration with STAND GHANA for a COVID-19 RELIEF DONATION OUTREACH on August 29th , 2021.

Through the kind support & donations from KASAF, it was a successful distribution of free food stuffs to several single mothers at Atabu in the Hohoe Municipality on Sunday, 29th August 2021. Stand Ghana and all beneficiaries greatly appreciate this timely intervention from KASAF.
