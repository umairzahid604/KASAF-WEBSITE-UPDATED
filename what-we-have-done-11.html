What We Have Done

WHAT WE HAVE DONE

KASAF-LAGOS: FREE 2 DAYS MEDICAL OUTREACH, ALIMOSHO LGA, LAGOS, Nigeria on the 7th and 8th of April, 2021 .

In commemoration of the World Health Day 2021, KASAF-LAGOS VOLUNTEERS embarked on a free 2 days Medical checkup for the people of Alimosho LGA of Lagos. Nigeria. Free medical check-up for blood pressure levels, glucose levels and free consultation was offered. Free medications such as Anti-malaria treatments, antibiotics, antihypertensive medications, anti-diabetic medications, pain medications, Ulcer medications were given out. It was an amazing time of service to our community, where we offered free medical check-up and distributed free medications to 200 individuals including children. Thank you dear Volunteers and partners for joining us in giving hope to our community. God bless you .


KASAF-LAGOS in collaboration with MAKOKO DREAM FOUNDATION: FREE 1 DAY MEDICAL OUTREACH, MAKOKO COMMUNITY, LAGOS, NIGERIA on Saturday 10th April, 2021.

In commemoration of the World Health Day 2021, KASAF-LAGOS collaborated with MAKOKO DREAM FOUNDATION and offered FREE Medical checkup for the people of MAKOKO COMMUNITY, Lagos. Nigeria. Free medical check-up for blood pressure levels, glucose levels and free consultation was offered. Free medications such as Anti-malaria treatments, antibiotics, antihypertensive medications, anti-diabetic medications, pain medications, Ulcer medications were given out. A total of 350 persons were attended to. It was a record breaking event and indeed an amazing time of service to our community. The children weren't left out, as biscuits were also distributed to over 150 kids present. Thank you dear Volunteers and partners for joining us in giving hope to our community. God bless you .


KASAF BWARI-ABUJA SCHOOL OUTREACH on April 13th 2021.

On April 13th 2021, KASAF BWARI-ABUJA volunteers performed a School outreach program at Government Day Secondary school, Bwari. Books, pencils, pens and writing materials were distributed to 37 orphans. Words of encouragement were also spoken to motivate them unto greater heights irrespective of the current situation report. The top 2 students received a tuition scholarship for 1 year and the funds were immediately transferred to the school's bank account. This was done to encourage them and other students present. It was indeed a great time of blessing for the students.
