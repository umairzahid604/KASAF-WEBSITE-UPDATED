WHAT WE HAVE DONE

KASAF ABUJA: 2 DAYS FREE MEDICAL OUTREACH, BARANGONI, BWARI-ABUJA on the 15th and 16th April, 2021.

KASAF ABUJA concluded a 2 days Medical Outreach at Barangoni Village. Two locations were utilized: Joshua Generation International church and Barangoni Village. Over the course of the 2 days, 350 individuals were attended to. Free medical check-up was performed and they were given free medications such as antibiotics, anti-diabetics, anti-malaria, pain medications and antihypertensives. Food packs (rice, indomie, garri, water) and water were also distributed to all present. It was indeed a great time of Refreshment as we blessed our community with the liquid love of Christ. God bless our Volunteers .


KASAF @ 1 AWARDS CEREMONY.April 6th 2021 marks 1 year since inception of KASAF (Kenny & Sandra Aruleba Foundation).

Starting with food distribution of 100 food packs at Skid row, Downtown Los Angeles, California, USA during the lockdown period because of the Pandemic, KASAF till date is celebrating giant strides of Impact and Achievements.   As of April 30th 2021, KASAF had completed a total of 31 Outreaches across 5 Countries and 20 cities, distributed over 4,000 meal packs to individuals and families, including women, men, children, orphans and underprivileged with over 105 volunteers.   Our volunteers are the KASAF we all see. Thank you dear volunteers for your selfless commitment to the growth and impact of KASAF globally. We appreciate you always and God bless you immensely.   KASAF AWARDS & CELEBRATIONS CEREMONY 2021 held in Abuja, Nigeria on Saturday 17th of April 2021. It was an avalanche of celebration as we appreciated and celebrated our volunteers and staff for their immense contributions to our work In KASAF worldwide over the last 1 year. Thank you dear Volunteers. God bless you


KASAF-PORT HARCOURT FOOD DISTRIBUTION OUTREACH, ENEKA TOWN, PORT HARCOURT, NIGERIA on 19th April 2021.

The food distribution exercise at Eneka Town was a huge success as we prepared and distributed food packs to 75 families in the Eneka area of Port Harcourt. Each pack contained rice, indomie, garrie and onions. It was indeed a great time of blessing to our community. Thank you dear volunteers. God bless you
