Global Analysis

Roughly 40 million people are experiencing food insecurity in the United States (Layne, 2019).

3.8 million People are facing food-insecurity in Nigeria (Action Against Hunger, 2019).

14.5 million Kenyans face food insecurity and poor nutrition each year (Schalit, 2020).

Approximately 51% of Ghanaians (approximately 14.8 million people) lack money to buy food (Mulangu, 2017).

41.4 percent of Ugandans were found to be undernourished in 2018, that is, Uganda having a Global Hunger Index (GHI) of 41.2%, and the rate continues to increase steadily due to increase in population from refugees from South Sudan and the Democratic Republic of the Congo (Global Hunger Index, 2019).
