What We Have Done

WHAT WE HAVE DONE

KASAF NASARAWA follow-up meeting at the IDP Camp, Gurku on Saturday 20th of February 2021.

Volunteers of KASAF NASARAWA, Nigeria met with the families of the young girls being mentored by KASAF. The families were encouraged and the young girls were motivated on the importance of keeping their hopes and dreams alive. It was a great time of impact for the girls and their families. Congratulations team. We are moving forward. God bless you


KASAF BWARI-ABUJA scholarship Award at L.E.A Yaro Memorial Primary School, Bwari on Thursday 4th of March 2021.

Focused on the mandate to ensure every child gets atleast basic education, KASAF paid the Tuition (PTA) of 60 Primary pupils and 20 Nursery Pupils (all orphans and underprivileged) at L.E.A YARO Memorial Primary School, Bwari, Abuja. This is in support of the 4th Sustainable Development Goal of the U.N of Quality Education. Thank you dear Sponsors, donors and volunteers. God bless you abundantly..


KASAF WELFARE FOOD OUTREACH @ USHAFA, BWARI-ABUJA on Saturday March 27th, 2021

It was an amazing time of blessing to 100 families who benefitted from the free food distribution exercise performed by volunteers of KASAF BWARI-ABUJA, Nigeria. Items distributed included Rice, garri (cassava meal), and indomie noodles. It was a great time of blessing to the Ushafa community. We are grateful to our amazing KASAF Volunteer Team members in Abuja who ensured this activity was a success. God bless you.
