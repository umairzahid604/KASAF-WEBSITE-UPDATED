What We Have Done

WHAT WE HAVE DONE

KASAF and STAND GHANA Christmas Food Drive 25th December 2020:

KASAF and STAND GHANA set out on December 25th 2020 to put smiles on the faces of 20 families in the Hohoe District of Ghana. It was a very emotional experience as we saw joy and gladness radiate from the faces of the recipients, especially following the devastating experience this year across the nations of the world. Thank you to our dear partners and sponsors for your awesome support always. God bless you.


KASAF IBADAN Outreach at Winnie's Castle Orphanage, Ibadan on November 24th, 2020

It was a great day at Winnie's Castle Orphanages as KASAF crew arrived the orphanage home of over 26 street, vulnerable and orphan children. Items such as toilet tissue, Noodles, biscuits, milo milk and sugar were donated to the home for the upkeep of the children. The children were very happy to see us and it sure did make a great impact in their lives. Thank you to our dear partners and sponsors. God bless you!
