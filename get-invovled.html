GET INVOLVED

JOIN US IN TOUCHING LIVES AND CHANGING THE WORLD

There are several ways to get involved:

1. Volunteer: If you are local to one of our locations, join forces with us during your spare time to be a positive impact to the community

2. Donate: Give towards our different projects and programs. All donations are Tax Deductible.

3. Connect us with Needy Communities & Individuals: Let us know of the needs in your area so we can reach out to help as much as we can. We currently do work in Los Angeles-California, Abuja-Nigeria, Lagos-Nigeria, Kampala-Uganda, Accra-Ghana, Nairobi-Kenya and Johannesburg-South Africa.

4. Become a KASAF Ambassador by sharing our work on your social media platforms with your contacts.
