What We Have Done

WHAT WE HAVE DONE

KASAF IBADAN WELFARE FOOD DISTRIBUTION, AJIBODE COMMUNITY, IBADAN on 23rd April 2021.

KASAF IBADAN Food Outreach was a great success with a total of 75 families at the Ajibode community blessed with food packs. Food items distributed were rice, indomie noodles and garri. We are grateful to God for the opportunity to serve our community. Thank you dearest volunteers for always being available to serve. God bless you


KASAF BARANGONI Volunteers Induction Meeting on May 9th 2021.

New and vibrant volunteers were inducted into the KASAF FAMILY on the 9th of May 2021 from Barangoni Village, Bwari-Abuja. These volunteers were trained on what KASAF stands for, what we do and how we become agents of change. It was indeed a great success. congratulations Our newest Team members, you guys are doing amazing. God bless you


KASAF NASARAWA WIDOW'S WELFARE OUTREACH, NEW KARU, NASARAWA STATE, NIGERIA on 15th of May 2021.

Saturday May 15th 2021 was yet another great day of blessing, as KASAF EDO volunteers set out to bless the community of Okhuesan, Evan South East with welfare packs of love. A total of 112 families were blessed with food packs containing rice, indomie and onions . Thank you dear Sponsors and volunteers for showing love one person at a time . God bless you If we all do a little, we can all do alot.


KASAF MPAPE-ABUJA, NIGERIA FOOD DISTRIBUTION On Thursday May 27th, 2021.

KASAF Volunteers in Mpape-Abuja, Nigeria set out to bless the people of Mapa Village with free food distribution to the community. A total of 110 food packs each containing items like rice, garri, noodles.   It was indeed a great time of blessing to the community. Many grateful recipients were recorded.   Thank you dear volunteers and sponsors for your great work of love to our communities.


KASAF IBADAN CHILDREN'S DAY CELEBRATIONS 2021

On Thursday 27th of May 2021, KASAF IBADAN volunteers were excited to celebrate Children's Day 2021 with pupils of IDC Model Pre Basic and Basic School, Owode, Apata. A total of 504 children were blessed with books, pens, pencils, drinks and biscuits. It was indeed a great time of celebration and joy for the children on this special day. Indeed all limits have been taken off. Thank you dear Volunteers, partners and sponsors for your great work. God bless you.


KASAF CAMEROON CHILDREN’S DAY CELEBRATION 2021

On Saturday May 29th 2021, Volunteers of KASAF Cameroon visited Home of Hope Orphanage, Ngembuh Village, North-West Region, in commemoration of Children’s Day 2021.   It was indeed a great time of fun with the 55 children present. Items donated included: rice, beans, popcorn, palm oil, savon, Omo powder detergent, garri, salt, Maggie, drinks, sweets and biscuits.   These items were greatly appreciated because of the shortage of food items in these affected zones due to the ongoing crises in Southern Cameroon.   Thank you dear volunteers and sponsors for your labor of Love. God bless you 


KASAF PORT HARCOURT Children's Day celebrations 2021.

On the 29th of May 2021, KASAF Port Harcourt volunteers celebrated children's day at David Bassey Ikpeme Foundation. It was indeed a great success . A total of 47 Children were blessed with food and relief items. The fun activities performed with the kids were amazing . Food items, toiletries and biscuits were distributed. Indeed it was a great time of blessing . Thank you dear Volunteers for your consistency in blessing our communities. God bless you
