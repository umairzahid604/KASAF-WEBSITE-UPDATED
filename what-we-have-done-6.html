What We Have Done

KASAF IBADAN, NIGERIA November 24th, 2020(Second Home):

KASAF IBADAN Outreach at Galilee Foundation Home, Ibadan on November 24th, 2020. It was a great day at Galilee Foundation Home as KASAF crew arrived the orphanage home of over 76 street, vulnerable and orphan children. Items such as toilet tissue, Noodles, biscuits, milo milk and sugar were donated to the home for the upkeep of the children. Thank you to our dear partners and sponsors for your kind heart. God bless you!



KASAF-KENYA ENTREPRENEURSHIP GRANT 2020:

6 Individuals were recipients of the grant in Mai Mahiu (hot waters), Naivasha County, KENYA.

1. Meet Agnes Muthoni. She is one of the recipients of KSh5,000 ($46). This grant will assist her in starting a beading business (Shanga work) to support her elderly aunt whom she lives with. She is a disabled orphan.

Thank you dearest partners and sponsors for your immense support. God bless you.

2. Meet Jihan Wanjiku. She is one of the recipients of KSh5,000 ($46). This grant will help her start a beading (shanga) business to support herself and her 3 kids, who are currently out of school because of no school fees. She currently has no job so this new business help her provide for her family.

Thank you dearest partners and sponsors for your immense support. God bless you.

3. Meet Keziah Wambui. She is one of the recipients of KSh5,000 ($46). This grant will support her in starting a second-hand shoe business, as well as support her 4 kids, because she is a widow. Her kids are currently out of school because of no school fees. So she is very grateful for this grant, which will support herself and her family.

Thank you dearest partners and sponsors for your immense support. God bless you.

4. Meet Ann Njoki. She is one of the recipients of KSh5,000 ($46). This grant will assist her in starting a second-hand bags and clothing business to support her husband and take care of her 4 kids and provide accommodation and school fees for her kids.

Thank you dearest partners and sponsors for your immense support. God bless you.

5. Meet Milkah Njeri. She is one of the recipients of KSh5,000 ($46). This grant will help her start a charcoal business to assist her in taking care of her 3 kids, provide for them and send them to school because she is a widow. So this grant comes in very handy.

Thank you dearest partners and sponsors for your immense support. God bless you.

6. Meet Mary Waithira. She is one of the recipients of KSh 5,000 ($46). This grant will help her start her bed sheets business. It will really help her to have a steady source of income to provide for her family.

Thank you dearest partners and sponsors for your immense support. God bless you.
